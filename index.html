Our Mission

To bring out each member's creativity, express thoughts and feelings through the written word, build a community of writing lovers, and connect closely through the art of writing.

Our Vision

To inspire every writing lover to follow their dream of creating and sharing stories. After 50 stories, we'll compile our works into a PDF book and publish our art, showing how words can connect hearts and create new worlds.
